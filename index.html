What is BooTcoDE?

BooTcoDE is a study group initiative which will use sololearn group chat as its platform! The idea is classify the members into different levels and give the challenges based on their levels. There will be one challenge for each level per week.These weekly challenges will help members at each level upgrade their skills. More features will be added!

What we need?

The Fighters: No initiative will be successfull without strong participation! We need you! Yes you! It doesnt matter if you are good in coding or you are going to begin! all it matters is that you need the passion for it! Join The Study Group!

The Ninjas: We need people who have atleast interediate knowledge in web technologies! You will be assigned with job of giving challenge to one of the level of fighters. You have to find one challenge suitable for your fighters per week! Show some spirit Ninjas. (Ninjas can participate in solving challenges!)

Levels For Fighters

Level 1: Starter, no knowledge in any of web technologies.

Level 2: HTML Beginner, Have a basic knowledge on HTML.

Level 3: CSS Starter, Have good knowledge in HTML,Starter in CSS.

Level 4: CSS Beginner, Have basic knowledge in HTML and CSS.

Level 5: JS Starter, Have good knowledge in HTML and CSS, Starter in JS.

Level 6: JS Beginner, Have a basic knowledge in HTML, CSS, JS.
