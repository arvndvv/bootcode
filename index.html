What is BooTcoDE?

BooTcoDE is a study group initiative which will use sololearn group chat as its platform! The idea is classify the members into different levels and give the challenges based on their levels. There will be one challenge for each level per week.These weekly challenges will help memebrs at each level upgrade their skills. More features will be added!
